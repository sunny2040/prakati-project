Awareness Programs For Child Labor

Child labor robs children of their innocence and potential, forcing them into a life of hardship instead of a life of learning and play. It’s our responsibility to ensure that every child is given the chance to grow up in a safe, nurturing environment. By raising awareness and taking action, we can help end child labor and give children the opportunities they deserve for a brighter future. Together, we can make a difference and protect the rights of every child
